f.a.q.

  • Q: Do you take design projects?

    A: I do! You should remember, though, that I am an artist that occasionally does design. Not a designer.

  • Q: What payment methods are accepted?

    A: I can take PayPal and Xoom.

  • Q: How long can I be expected to wait?

    A: Depending on what happens irl and the scale of the project, you can expect to wait from 4 days to 1 week. No guarantees!

  • Q: Why are there long periods between responses?

    A: It could either be that I'm preoccupied with life, or that our timezones don't align. I'm free from 10:30-23:30 ICT.

  • Q: I'd like to use your art commercially, what should I know?

    A: Unless you have already paid for a commercial license, you aren't allowed to use my artworks commercially. Note, commercial licenses' prices can be adjusted for your use case(s).

  • Q: I have some adjustments I'd like to make...

    A: After finalization, I can only do 1 revisions for free. For more revisions, you can expect to be charged 10% of your item's price on top of the original price. Invoices will be separate.

  • Q: May I use one of your personal works for something of mine? I don't need to commission you.

    A: Absolutely! Only for personal purposes, though. If you want to use them commercially, please purchase a commercial license for each piece you want to license. Note: I may turn down some requests for, well, personal reasons.
